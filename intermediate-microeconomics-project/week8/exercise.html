Week 8 – Exercises

Interactive exercises where students manipulate the Week 2 graphs themselves.

How to use these exercises

These activities are designed for short in-class tasks or homework. Each graph usually comes with a small set of questions or prompts (which you can host in LMS / PDF / or the video description).

You can also run them live: set a slider value, ask students what happens, then reveal the answer by moving the slider.
